Emergency information

This page offers general information. For urgent concerns contact a veterinarian or emergency animal hospital immediately.

Warning signs
  • Difficulty breathing, collapse, or unresponsiveness
  • Severe bleeding or deep wounds
  • Repeated vomiting, severe diarrhea, or signs of distress
  • Seizures, severe pain, or sudden paralysis
What NOT to feed dogs
  • Chocolate, caffeine, or xylitol (found in some gums and candies)
  • Grapes or raisins
  • Onions, garlic, sweets, or large amounts of salt
When to visit a vet

If you notice persistent abnormal behavior, bleeding, trouble breathing, or anything that worsens quickly — seek professional care.
